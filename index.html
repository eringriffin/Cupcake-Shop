Get the GOODNESS of Sweet Treats Baking!

Welcome to our kitchen! Please note we are not a storefront bakery but love to see clients by appointment.

Mom used to make us eat cookies with oats and nuts, wheat germ and whole wheat flour. We thought she was nuts herself, but now that we are grown up, we recognize the need to introduce wholesome ingredients in our baked goods.

We make delicious cakes, cupcakes, and desserts for weddings, special events, and wholesale baking!

We incorporate locally sourced ingredients of local butter, veg-fed cage-free local chicken eggs, local organic field lavender, whole wheat flour, flaxseed and wheat germ, farmers market berries and nuts, and top quality purees and chocolates.

Sweet Cheeks Baking Co. is a sustainable women-owned business baking retail and wholesale desserts locally for San Diego and shipping across the country.

Sometimes the simple things in life are the best. Our signature flavor (and most popular!) is our Sugar Cupcake. We promise you it will be the best thing you've ever put in your mouth!

Other flavors:

  • Salted Caramel
  • Peppermint
  • Pumpkin
  • Sicilian Pistachio
